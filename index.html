Biography

Tian Li is an Associate Professor at Department of Energy and Process Engineering, Norwegian University of Science and Technology. He is a part of the ComKin Group, which applies modern numerical methods and experimental studies to problems arising in combustion systems, with a special emphasis on clean and sustainable energy production.
